Chicken Biryani

Cooking: 60 min
Prep: 10 min
Serves: 2-3
Cooking: 60 min
Prep: 10 min
Serves: 2-3

Chicken Biryani is one of my most favorite dishes and definitely one of the most requested meals at my house. We often pair it with cooling raita and a squeeze of fresh lime for a hearty meal.

There are many variations of this recipe that originate in several states of India. My favorite, of course, is the one made by my mom that I grew up eating. Warm, deep spiced Chicken Biryani, with freshly ground spices, caramelized onions, marinated tender chicken cooked over long grain basmati rice!

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup ghee (clarified butter)
  • 20 whole cloves
  • 9 whole cardamom pods
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 5 bay leaves
  • 5 small green chile pepper
  • 2 tablespoons ginger garlic paste
  • 1 (3 pound) whole chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 ½ cups plain yogurt
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 6 fresh curry leaves (Optional)
  • 1 Kg Basmati Rice
  • Water

Preparations

  1. To marinate the chicken for the biryani, combine the vegetable oil, garlic, ginger, chili peppers, mint, cilantro, garam masala, cinnamon and salt in a large bowl and stir together. Add the chicken pieces and toss together making sure the chicken is thoroughly coated in the marinade. Allow the chicken to marinate for at least 1 hour or up to overnight.

  2. Meanwhile add 1 kg of Basmati Rice in large pot and rinse it properly.Drain and soak in fresh water and drain it.Drain and soak in fresh water and drain it.

  3. Prepare Chicken

    • Heat ghee or oil in pot (Make sure you use large one for this).

    • Add 1 bay leaf
    • Add 4 green cardamom
    • Add 6 cloves
    • Add half teaspoon of jeera
    • Add thinly sliced onions and fry them on a medium heat until light brown

  4. Add marinated chicken and saute until it becomes pale for 5 minutes.

  5. Lower the flame and cook untill chicken is soft and completely cooked.

  7. Taste the chicken and add salt if needed.

  8. Prepare biryani.

    • Mix everything well and spread it evenly in a single layer
    • Layer drained rice all over the chicken.Add 3 cups of water (Add water according to rice needed to cooked)
    • Level the rice gently on top.Do not mix up everything
    • Cover the pot and cook on medium heat until rice is done completely.

  9. Check whether the biryani is cooked properly or not.
